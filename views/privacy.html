GlobalScentsations' Privacy Policy

in GlobalScentsations we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you visit our international website and purchase our products.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information:

    • We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information when you place an order or register on our website.

  • Non-Personal Information:

    • We also collect non-personal information such as your browser type, device information, IP address, and browsing behavior to improve our services and your shopping experience.

    • Non-personal information helps us analyze website traffic, enhance our website design, and develop new products to better serve you.

  • Third-Party Service Providers:

    • We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, delivering products, and performing other business functions. These providers are obligated to protect your information and use it solely for the services they provide to us.

4. Your Rights

  • Access and Correction:

    • You have the right to access and correct any personal information we hold about you. You can update your information by logging into your account or contacting our customer service team.

  • Data Deletion:

    • You may request the deletion of your personal information by contacting us. Please note that we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

5. Security Measures

  • Protection of Information:

    • We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. This includes encryption, firewalls, and secure servers.
